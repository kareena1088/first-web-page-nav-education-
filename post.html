The Naveducation Program: Why and How

Q1 Why Join Naveducation?

  1. Find Purpose: Tackle education inequity and inspire change.
  2. Lead: Develop leadership skills while empowering learners.
  3. Be the Change: Impact lives and transform communities.

Q2 What Does the Naveducation Program Entail?

  1. Teach and Empower: Support learners' academic and personal growth while engaging parents and stakeholders.
  2. Lead and Reflect: Develop leadership skills through daily work, coaching, and feedback.
  3. Drive Change: Launch initiatives to solve real challenges in education.

Leadership Development, Training, and Readiness

Q1 How is Naveducation Different?

Naveducation goes beyond academics, focusing on holistic growth and community impact. We see teaching as leadership, building skills and mindsets that empower learners and mentors to become changemakers.

Q2 Is Prior Teaching Experience Required?

No, prior teaching experience is not needed. Naveducation provides comprehensive training to equip you with the skills and knowledge to excel as a mentor and leader.

Q3 Will I Be Adequately Trained?

Yes, Naveducation provides thorough training through an immersive program covering curriculum design, lesson planning, classroom management, and student assessment.

  1. Embark on a leadership journey to develop your strengths and stakeholder engagement.
  2. Receive ongoing support from a dedicated mentor to guide your growth.
  3. Gain hands-on experience as a full-time mentor with continuous on-the-job training.
